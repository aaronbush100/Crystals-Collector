Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the competition.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you'll add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to randomly generated number. You lose the game if your total score goes higher than the randomly generated number.


The value of each crystal is hidden until it's clicked.


Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will change and the random number will change.
